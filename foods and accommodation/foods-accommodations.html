Maya Homes

Maya Homes along Valencia Road, Dumaguete invites you to indulge in a spacious 3‑bedroom haven with cool air‑conditioned comfort, secure parking, free WiFi, a sleek modern kitchen, laundry ease, and a private balcony—your perfect blend of convenience and relaxation in one unforgettable stay.

Tejero Highland Resort & Adventure Park

Tejero Highland Resort & Adventure Park in Valencia, Negros Oriental is a mountainside escape where natural spring pools, thrilling water slides, ziplining, and ATV rides meet cool highland air, lush greenery, and cozy cottages—making it the ultimate spot for families, friends, and groups to enjoy both adventure and relaxation in one unforgettable getaway.

Pulangbato Falls Mountain Resort

Pulangbato Falls Mountain Resort in Valencia, Negros Oriental is a breathtaking hideaway where a striking red‑rock waterfall plunges into a cool natural pool, surrounded by lush greenery, cozy cottages, and easy trails—an inviting escape for families, friends, and nature lovers seeking both relaxation and adventure in one unforgettable spot.

Romantic Eco Sanctuary

Romantic Eco Sanctuary is an off‑grid mountain retreat in Purok Durian, Cambucad, Valencia, Dumaguete. Surrounded by nature, it offers free WiFi, secure parking, a golf course, pool views, sauna, kitchen, and bar—perfect for a peaceful and rejuvenating escape.

Belfort House B&B

Located at Km. 8 Jose Romero Road, East Balabag, Valencia, Dumaguete, this property offers free WiFi, balcony or terrace access, express check‑in and check‑out, secure parking, spacious family rooms, and a pet‑friendly environment—perfect for a comfortable and convenient stay.

Chosen’s Cafe

A cozy highland café in Valencia, Dumaguete, offering scenic views, cool mountain air, and a warm atmosphere built from patience, hard work, and family dedication.

Cafe Le Valencia

Cafe le Valencia in Valencia, Negros Oriental is a charming café‑restaurant where greenery, a cozy vibe, and a menu of pizza, pasta, burgers, rice meals, coffee, milk tea, and desserts come together—making it the go‑to spot for locals and travelers to relax, dine, and recharge after exploring the town’s waterfalls and mountain attractions.

Sibaan sa Valencia

A delicious food hub in Valencia, Negros Oriental, it’s best known for its palangking lechon and savory tuhog‑tuhog. This local favorite brings authentic flavors and a true taste of Filipino comfort food.

Spicy Food House

An inviting spot in Valencia with an outdoor charcoal grill, serving Indian, Thai, Italian, and more. Formerly Spice Café and Restaurant, now relocated nearby, it continues to offer delicious global flavors.

S. Bakery and Coffee

S. Bakery and Coffee is a modern café offering affordable yet quality coffee and baked snacks. The space is calming and peaceful, with an elegant vibe that feels delightfully upscale.
